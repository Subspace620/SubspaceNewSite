Admiral Amanda Blunt

Club Position: Fleet Admiral - Extended Exe
Service Record:
Joined club September 1998
Voted to executive council, extended, by fellow executives, Oct. 1998
Officially assumed executive position Oct. 1998; promoted to full Lt.
Promoted to Lt-Commander (Secretary/Treasurer) in November 1998.
Promoted to Captain (Club President) in March 1999.
Promoted to Admiral in April 2005 (after 6 YEARS of Presidency)
Promoted to Fleet Admiral in April 2006 (To honour her 6 YEARS of Presidency)
Assimilated into the Star Trek Universe, due to parental units, not much is known about her. Just transferred from the U.S.S. McGill. Likes to haggle with Ferengi and that she has frequent flyer mileage to Sickbay (You name it, she's broken it, bruised it, sprained it, torn it and pulled it) and is proud of it. Was promoted to Lt. Commander Jody's position after Commander Jo asked for a demotion and Jody became Vice- President.

Captain Blunt commanded SubSpace for 4 years before getting her own support ship the USS Costanza NCC-200304. Which she valiantly commanded for 2 more years before being promoted to Admiral in April 2005.

The reasons for the promotion depend on who you ask. Starfleet states that it was due to her exlempary service, while the crew claims that it was to to preserve their sanity from not only her 'pythonisms', but to keep her from doing more damage to the computer systems. She is still around but only in an advisory role. Her future plans include converting many youngsters to Pythonism.
