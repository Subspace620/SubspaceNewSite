Station Activities

Club Events include:

  • General Meetings (to Procrastinate and make plans for outings)
  • Outings include: Move Nights, Premieres, Marathons/Parties, Board Game nights, Laser Quest and Bowling where you could win the Locutus of Bowl trophy.
    • This year we have been doing weekly movie nights and are starting up a monthly games night. Check our google groups page or the news section for more information.
  • At the start of every school year we run a potluck barbeque in Emily Murphy park. For a map to the park and other info on the barbeque, Click Here.
  • The club also runs a few RPGs
  • Whatever MEMBERS wanna do......the club is a member run group so if you wanna do something let the Command staff know or post it to the mailing list!
