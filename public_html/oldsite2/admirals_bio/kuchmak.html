Background
Chris has been around the fleet and back.  When he joined Starfleet nobody in his family would support him. He has since proved himself by taking vacations in the mountainous regions of Crow's-nest Pass on Impeesa Prime.  He has had an exemplary record aboard Subspace 620 moving up in the ranks quickly.

His future plans include returning to Starfleet Academy and teaching the future of Starfleet. As well as making the Station itself more than just a backwater in the Univas Albertus system.  Nearby is Vulcan which he has already visited once with intentions to return.

He likes the primitive games of hockey, football, and soccer; and he enjoys a good beer... preferably a Thirsty Beaver.
