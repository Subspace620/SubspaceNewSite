Background

Jared joined the starship Subspace 620 immediately after moving to the Univas Albertus System. Even as an
Ensign Jared was praised for his enthusiasm. The starship had fallen into a routine of one movie mission per
week and Jared saw room for improvement. He realized that the ship had potential to be used for much more.

During his first year he organized the first games mission and gained the nickname "games night guy". Starfleet
realized that this was this kind of progress that the starship 620 needed and they promptly promoted him to Captain
at the beginning of his second year. His two years as Captain marked an age of expansion for the club. He first
added weekly games missions to the club, then he added weekly book missions to the club. By the end of this reign
as Captain, the ship was busier than ever and well known among the Univas Albertus System. In 2013 he was promoted
to Admiral but continues to help advise the crew of the starship Subspace 620 to this day.
