Background

NPC Notes:

"Colleen" is a young human wizard who can be encountered by the players in the basement museums of the U of A's Earth Science Building (see dungeon map 12, page 57) during any quest that takes them there. She is friendly, if bumbling, and perpetually covered in ink from working in her spellbook. She has been a member of the adventurers' guild 'Subspace 620' since 2019. Immediately upon encountering the party, she will offer to identify any rocks they may have in their inventory.

Colleen can be convinced to act as a guide for the party through the rest of the EAS dungeon with a DC 13 persuasion check, and will further accompany them into the CCIS, Chemistry or Biosci dungeons with a DC 15 roll. Add a +5 bonus to the roll if the players have given her a rock to identify, or if they have previously completed the 'RPGs with Subspace 620' sidequest.
