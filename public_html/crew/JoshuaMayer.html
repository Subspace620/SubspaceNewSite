Background

Hi there, I'm Joshua. I joined the club here back in Fall of 2019 upon my third year of my degree and first year transferring. I joined the club in hopes of finding a D&D game to join and make friends. Having made a lot of great friends I offered to help out with organizing the club and thus was asked to take on the position of a Lieutenant. This club means a lot to me, introducing me to a lot of my friends. I like to help out with the club as if gives me an opportunity to meet up with friends and give back to the club.
