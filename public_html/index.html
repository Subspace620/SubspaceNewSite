Subspace 620

U of A Science Fiction and Fantasy Club

Who are we?

Subspace 620 is a social club at the University of Alberta whose area of focus is the realm of Science Fiction and Fantasy. Before Corona virus, our events include Dungeons & Dragons once a week, game nights on Fridays, and occasional parties.

When Corona virus became serious last March, we moved everything online. From core events like Board Games and D&D, to atmosphere like casual conversation and homework help, everything is on our Discord server.

What are we up to?

We have 6 steady RPG groups. Our book club is alive again for the first time in years! We're reading Graceling by Kristen Cashore.

We also have random board game nights every week or so. Get the @boardgames role in the server to be notified when we play games!
